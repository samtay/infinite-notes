Charles “C.T.” Tavis

Hal’s uncle; Avril’s step-brother (possibly half-brother). Possibly Mario’s father (pp. 451). Headmaster at ETA. Mother was the quasi-homodontic Elizabeth Tavis, Mr. Mondragon’s nth wife, whom he married in Vermont (pp. 901).
